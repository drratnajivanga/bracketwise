The system

Research that knows your tax bracket.

Most investment tools ignore the single biggest drag on returns — taxes. I try not to. Every observation is filtered for your tax slab.

01

Factor screening

I screen NSE 500 stocks daily using a personal factor model. Automated gates filter expensive and low-quality stocks — protecting against the most common return-destroying mistakes for high-bracket investors.

02

Tax bracket filter

At 30% slab, every signal is reviewed for LTCG eligibility. Dividend stocks flagged as inefficient (taxed at 30% for you). F&O excluded entirely. Your after-tax return is the only metric that matters.

03

Macro overlay

Crude oil, dollar index, India VIX and FII flows scanned every morning. When VIX is elevated, recommended position sizes automatically reduce by 30%. Brief arrives at 7:30 AM before markets open.

Tax bracket intelligence

One size never fit all investors.

The same stock can be a good or bad investment depending on your tax slab. Here is how the same stock looks different across brackets.

Growth investors
0–5% slab
Income below ₹7L
  • STCG tolerable at 20% — more flexibility
  • Momentum and growth stocks suitable
  • Small/midcap allocation up to 30%
  • Tax is not the primary constraint
Balanced investors
20% slab
Income ₹7L–₹15L
  • LTCG strongly preferred over STCG
  • Quality + growth combination ideal
  • Avoid F&O — taxed at slab rate
  • Annual LTCG harvest in February
High-income professionals
30% slab
Income above ₹15L
  • LTCG at 12.5% vs STCG 20% — critical
  • No dividend stocks — taxed at 30%
  • No F&O — business income at 30%
  • NPS 80CCD(2) — 14% of basic deductible
  • ESOP: exercise only at liquidity event
  • ₹1.25L free exemption resets every April
