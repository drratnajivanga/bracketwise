1. Who we are

BracketWise is an AI-generated equity research platform operated by Virat Ventures, based in Bengaluru, India. Our website is located at bracketwise.in.

For privacy-related queries, contact us at: privacy@bracketwise.in

BracketWise is NOT registered with the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) as an Investment Adviser or Research Analyst. All content is for informational purposes only.

2. Data we collect

Information you provide

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our weekly research brief
  • Tax bracket preference — if you select your income bracket on the website (stored locally in your browser only, not on our servers)

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, referring URL
  • IP address — collected automatically by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and analytics services
  • Cookies — see Section 4 for details

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, address, or any financial account details
  • We do not collect your PAN, Aadhaar, or any government ID
  • We do not collect your portfolio holdings or trading history

3. How we use your data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Email address — to send you the weekly BracketWise research brief you subscribed to, and occasional product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.
  • Usage data — to understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it, and to measure the reach of our content.
  • Analytics — to understand our audience and improve the quality of our research content.

We will never use your email address to send unsolicited commercial communications unrelated to BracketWise. We will never sell your email address to third parties.

4. Cookies and advertising

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies we use

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics). These do not personally identify you.
  • Advertising cookies — we use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google AdSense and advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our website, visit Google's privacy policy.

Managing cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our site.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use third-party services to operate our platform (email delivery, analytics, website hosting). These providers access your data only to perform services on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose your data if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfer — if Virat Ventures is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Third-party services we use include:

  • GitHub Pages (website hosting) — GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Analytics (usage analytics) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense (advertising) — Google Privacy Policy

6. Your rights under DPDP Act 2023

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — you can request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can withdraw consent for data processing at any time (e.g., unsubscribe from our email list)
  • Right to grievance redressal — you can raise a grievance with us regarding processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bracketwise.in. We will respond within 30 days.

To unsubscribe from our weekly brief, click the unsubscribe link in any email we send you, or email us at privacy@bracketwise.in with subject "Unsubscribe".

7. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We retain your email address only for as long as you are subscribed to our newsletter. Upon unsubscription, your email will be removed from our mailing list within 30 days.

8. Children's privacy

BracketWise is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@bracketwise.in and we will delete that information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
